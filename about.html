About us

Philippine Cashtrout Lending Corporation .

Philippine Cashtrout Lending Corporation is one of the first consumer lending companies in the philippines that provide mobile-based credit services with an online application, KYC, credit investigation and loan issuance. The company has developed proprietary algorithms and formulates as ‘Social Worth’ score (SW score) basis of robust social media underwriting systems to provide the best experience to users. Philippine Cashtrout Lending Corporation is a duly registered corporation holding a Certificate of Authority to Operate as a Lending Company issued by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in the Philippines.
